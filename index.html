About Us

Who Are We?

AKUMELS is a unique group of Medical Laboratory Students at Kenyatta University, united by our shared passion for advancing the field of medical diagnostics. We are driven by the collective need to deliver proper and accurate patient diagnosis practices, ensuring that the foundation of healthcare is built on precision and reliability.

Our Mission

To deliver accurate patient diagnostics while boosting academic standards at Kenyatta University. By applying theory to practice, promoting ethics, and offering exposure through research and community service, we shape future leaders in medical laboratory science.

Our Vision

To lead the advancement of medical laboratory science by empowering our members to make meaningful contributions to healthcare. We aim to cultivate ethical, well-rounded professionals who drive innovation, research, and community service to improve public health.
